About this platform

This interactive platform displays daily Canadian Fire Weather Index (FWI) conditions and records for active weather stations across Canada.

Colored markers identify stations currently experiencing daily, monthly or all-time FWI-related records.

Click on a station to access interactive time series, historical comparisons and operational weather forecast products.
